# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and avoid personal attacks.
- Accept feedback gracefully and focus on project outcomes.
- Help maintain a welcoming and inclusive environment.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Trolling, insults, and derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is officially representing the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may take fair corrective action for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Reporting

Please report incidents privately via the support channel described in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md). Provide links, screenshots, and context where possible.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Consequences

Possible consequences include warning, temporary ban from collaboration channels, or permanent ban from the project, depending on severity and recurrence.
